### The Architecture of Deception

In decentralized finance, we engineer trustless systems under the assumption that mathematics cannot be compromised. We spend millions auditing smart contracts for reentrancy, formally verifying state transitions, and locking down the protocol layer. 

But the attack surface has shifted. The most devastating exploits in Web3 bypass the cryptography entirely to target **Layer 8: The Human Protocol**.

**Project Sentry** is an active threat intelligence feed and research initiative dedicated to deconstructing the intersection of Web3 security and cyberpsychology. 

Adversaries are no longer just hacking code; they are systematically hacking cognitive biases. They weaponize authority hijacking, the sunk cost fallacy, and the operant conditioning inherent in DeFi to execute complex social engineering funnels. By analyzing these Web2-to-Web3 data harvesting operations, identity arbitrage tactics, and malicious templates, Project Sentry aims to map the psychological mechanics of modern on-chain exploits.

Security is not just about writing flawless code or understanding blockchain infrastructure. It is about deeply understanding the psychological vulnerabilities of the users interacting with the network, and engineering systems that protect the human API.

Assume compromise. Verify everything.
