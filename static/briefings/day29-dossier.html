Part 3: The Real-Life Impact

"I don't own stocks or bonds, so why should I care?" You care because the Crowding Out effect dictates your reality. When the government vacuums up all the money, private businesses starve.

Job Stagnation

If a company cannot attract investment because everyone is busy lending money to the government, that company cannot expand. If they cannot expand, they cannot hire you. The "lack of jobs" isn't a mystery; it is a direct mathematical result of the government hoarding capital.

The Innovation Death Spiral

Businesses need cheap loans to innovate. But because the government has set the baseline interest rate so high, commercial banks will charge private businesses 20% to 25% for a loan. Very few legal businesses can survive those margins, leading to a stagnant, uncreative economy.
